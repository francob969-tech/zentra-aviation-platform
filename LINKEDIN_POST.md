✈️ New project: a client acquisition platform for a private air taxi operator.

When a private aviation operator starts out, the problem isn't advertising.
It's that **inquiries get lost** — they come in through a stray WhatsApp, a call,
a referral, and without a system, they go cold.

To solve it, at ZENTRA Studio we didn't build "a website." We built a complete
commercial system:

🎯 A premium site with executive aviation aesthetics.
📩 Dual-capture lead generation: every inquiry opens in WhatsApp and is saved to the system — even if the client never sends it.
🗂️ A private panel (mini-CRM) where the operator sees each request, tracks its status and replies via WhatsApp in one click. With auto-refresh.
⏱️ A calculator that translates the service into what the client is really buying: time.
🗺️ An animated route map and a dedicated aircraft page, with prudent copy suited to a regulated sector.

The website is the visible surface. Underneath runs the capture and follow-up.
